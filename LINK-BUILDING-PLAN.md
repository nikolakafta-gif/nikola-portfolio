## 4. Outreach Email Templates

### Template A: Broken Link Replacement

```
Subject: Quick fix for a broken link on [page title]

Hi [name],

I noticed a broken link on your [page title] page — [broken URL] returns a 404.

I have a similar resource that could work as a replacement: [your URL]. It covers [brief description].

Either way, wanted to flag the broken link. Hope it helps.

Nikola
```

### Template B: Resource Page Addition

```
Subject: Resource for your [topic] page

Hi [name],

I came across your [page title] and thought my guide on [topic] might be a useful addition for your readers: [your URL].

It covers [2-3 specific things the guide addresses] and is kept up to date.

No worries if it's not a fit. Thanks for putting together such a useful list.

Nikola
```

### Template C: Guest Post Pitch

```
Subject: Article idea for [site name]

Hi [name],

Let me know if you're interested.

Nikola
```
