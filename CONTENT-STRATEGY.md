## AEO Content Guidelines (Apply to Every Article)

1. **Answer-first intro**: First 2-3 sentences directly answer the title question
2. **Question-format H2s**: At least 50% of subheadings should be phrased as questions
3. **FAQ section**: 5-8 questions at the bottom, each answer 40-60 words, self-contained
4. **FAQPage schema**: JSON-LD markup for FAQ sections
5. **Author byline**: Link to homepage (acts as author page)
6. **Publish date**: Always include, keep updated
7. **External citations**: Link to 2-3 authoritative sources per article
